    Identify

    The first step to starting a successful business is identifying a need in the market. Our entrepreneurship cell believes in encouraging students to identify problems and challenges that can be addressed through innovative solutions. Through our programs and workshops, we help students develop the skills needed to identify opportunities and create value for customers.

    Inovate

    Innovation is at the heart of entrepreneurship. Our cell is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and creativity among students. We believe that innovation is not just about developing new products or services, but also about finding new and better ways to do things. Through our events and mentorship programs, we help students unleash their creativity and develop the mindset needed to drive innovation.

    Incubate

    Starting a business can be a daunting task, but with the right support, it can also be an exciting and rewarding experience. Our entrepreneurship cell provides a supportive and collaborative environment where students can incubate their ideas and turn them into viable businesses. We offer mentorship, networking opportunities, and access to resources that can help students take their ideas from concept to reality.

    Vission

    Empowering and inspiring the next generation of entrepreneurs by providing the resources, mentorship, and community needed to turn innovative ideas into successful businesses.

    The place where students become the entrepreneurs of tomorrow.

    Fostering a dynamic community of entrepreneurial thinkers, makers, and doers, driving innovation and creating impact for a better world.

    e-Entrepreneurship Cell, RGPV is a non-profit organization that believes entrepreneurship is pivotal for our country's development. It was established with the purpose of creating, fostering, and promoting the spirit of developing a culture of entrepreneurship in our surroundings. The team here works to encourage students to embrace the idea of starting their venture and dispel the fear of starting a startup. We guide young entrepreneurs and technopreneurs in their journey from campus to incubators and support them in transforming their budding ideas into successful startups.
